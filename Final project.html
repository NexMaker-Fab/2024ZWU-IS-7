*Project Concept
With the rise of self-media and the emergence of rolling media, the spread of information has become more subjective, and various values ​​and ideas are flooding the entire network, making it difficult for everyone to distinguish between right and wrong. In addition, the current social situation of accelerated pace of life has made everyone "scroll" the media faster and faster, and sometimes they unconsciously acquiesce to online violence and various negative thoughts. When you browse this information, you think you can control your own thoughts, but in fact, you can't escape it anyway and will inevitably be affected by it.
We hope everyone can get out of this infinite scrolling.

*Interaction Effects
Through touch screen interaction, users stand in front of our touch screen and control the changes of screen content with their fingers, similar to swiping video comments. The speed at which information appears on the screen accumulates over time. All negative information will be accumulated in the container behind the device in the form of black balls. After a certain number of balls in the screen container, the balls will be ejected to display all the negative information the user has seen during this process.

Material we use
*Clear plastic pipe and plastic ball
Plastic is a widely used synthetic polymer material with good plasticity and flowability, which can be processed and formed through heating softening and cooling hardening processes.
Material composition
Plastics are mainly composed of additives such as synthetic resins, fillers, plasticizers, stabilizers, and colorants.
Synthetic resin is the main component of plastics and plays a skeletal role in plastics.
Additives are used to improve the properties of plastics, such as strength, stability, color, etc.
Characteristics
1.Plastics have advantages such as light weight, insulation, and resistance to chemical corrosion.
2.It has strong plasticity and can be made into products of various shapes and sizes.Therefore, its product variety is diverse, such as the pipes and balls used in the project are all made of plastic.
3.Plastic has good water resistance, oil resistance, and drug resistance, but poor heat resistance and wear resistance.
4.Plastic has poor thermal and electrical conductivity, but its conductivity can be improved by adding conductive fillers.
Main types
Universal plastics such as polyethylene (PE), polypropylene (PP), polyvinyl chloride (PVC), etc. have a wide range of uses and low prices.
Engineering plastics such as polyamide (PA) and polycarbonate (PC) have superior performance and are suitable for special industrial environments.
Environmental effect
Because plastic is non degradable in the short term and toxic to the natural environment, we need to handle it properly after use and not dispose of it casually.
*PLA material printed gears
PLA， The full name is Polylactic Acid, which is a new type of biodegradable material, also known as Polylactide. It is a polyester polymer obtained through polymerization reaction using lactic acid as the main raw material.
Material characteristics
1. Biodegradability: PLA materials have good biodegradability and can be completely degraded by microorganisms in nature after use, ultimately generating carbon dioxide and water, which is environmentally friendly.
2. Thermal stability: PLA materials have good thermal stability, with a processing temperature range of 170~230 ° C and good solvent resistance.
3. Processability: PLA materials can be processed in various ways, such as extrusion, spinning, biaxial stretching, injection blow molding, etc.
Optical performance: PLA materials have good glossiness and transparency, and have unique advantages compared to other biodegradable products.
4. Breathability: PLA film has good breathability, oxygen permeability, and carbon dioxide permeability, as well as the ability to isolate odors.
Application
Medical field: used for manufacturing absorbable sutures, bone fixation materials, and implants and other medical devices.
Packaging field: make packaging bags, foam boxes, etc., effectively reduce environmental pollution.
3D printing: It is one of the most widely used materials in 3D printing, which is easy to process, cost-effective, and biodegradable.
In the agricultural field, it is used to manufacture agricultural film, which can be degraded into carbon dioxide and water without polluting soil and crops.
Textiles: PLA textiles have good softness and breathability, and can be used to make clothing and household textiles.
Daily necessities: used for manufacturing cups, tableware, and baby bottles, non-toxic, harmless, and environmentally friendly.
